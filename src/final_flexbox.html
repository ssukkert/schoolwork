Tips

Rather if you are right or left handed, and if your mark is the 2nd arrow, try to line up to when you roll the bowl it hits that arrow. For example, have your feet and shoulders angle to where you want the ball to go. Keep your shoulders square with the pins and have your feet angled where you want the ball to go, and follow through with your release, and you should improve your score greatly.
